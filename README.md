# Smart Reminder Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

## Information We Collect

* Phone number for account authentication
* Reminder information created by users
* Device information required for notifications
* Optional family tracking information

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

* Authenticate users
* Deliver reminder notifications
* Synchronize reminder data across devices
* Provide family tracking features
* Improve application performance and reliability

## Data Storage

Data may be securely stored and processed using Firebase services provided by Google.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be processed by service providers required for application functionality, including Firebase and Google Play services.

## Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect user information.

## Children's Privacy

Smart Reminder Pro is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

## Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the contact information provided on the Google Play listing.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
